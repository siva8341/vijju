Virat Kohli - Cricket Legend

Virat Kohli is widely regarded as one of the greatest cricketers of all time. Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, he has established himself as a modern cricket icon through his exceptional batting skills, leadership qualities, and passionate approach to the game.

Career Highlights:
- Former captain of the Indian cricket team across all formats
- One of the most successful batsmen in international cricket
- Known for his aggressive batting style and chase mastery
- Has broken numerous batting records in all formats of the game
- Led India to historic Test series victories, including in Australia

Records and Achievements:
- Multiple ICC awards, including Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy for ICC Cricketer of the Year
- Fastest player to reach 10,000 ODI runs
- One of the few batsmen with an average of over 50 in all three formats of international cricket
- Holds the record for most centuries in ODI run-chases
- Known for his exceptional fitness standards that transformed Indian cricket

Off the field, Kohli is a successful entrepreneur with various business ventures including fashion, fitness, and restaurants. He is married to Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma, and they have a daughter named Vamika. 

Kohli's influence extends beyond cricket, as he has become a cultural icon and one of the most marketable athletes globally. His dedication to fitness and disciplined lifestyle has inspired millions of young athletes across the world.
